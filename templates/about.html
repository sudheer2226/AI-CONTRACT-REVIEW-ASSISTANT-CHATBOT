⚖️ AI Legal Contract Analyzer

Simplifying complex legal contracts using Artificial Intelligence

📌 What is this application?

This web application helps users understand legal contracts easily. Instead of reading long and complex legal documents, users can upload their contract and get a clear, simple explanation instantly.

⚙️ How does it work?

  • User registers and logs in securely
  • Uploads a contract (PDF / DOCX / TXT)
  • Selects preferred language
  • AI analyzes the contract
  • Shows risks, responsibilities, payments & termination clauses
  • User can ask questions and download the analysis

🧠 Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python Flask
  • Database: SQLite
  • AI Model: LegalBERT + AI Generator
  • Security: Password Hashing, Sessions

🎯 Why this project?

Legal documents are difficult for common people to understand. This project bridges the gap between legal language and users, making contract understanding simple, fast, and accessible.
